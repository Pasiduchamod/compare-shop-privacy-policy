# Privacy Policy for CompareShop

**Last updated:** 2025/12/25

CompareShop is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Information Collection

**CompareShop does NOT collect:**
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Photos, contacts, or media
- Payment or financial information

All data you enter (products, prices, notes, categories) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Internet Usage

CompareShop works **completely offline**.  
No data is sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

**CompareShop does not use:**
- Ads
- Analytics
- Third-party SDKs
- Trackers

## Children's Privacy

CompareShop does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, it is safe for all age groups.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page.
